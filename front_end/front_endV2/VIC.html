Victoria

Melbourne

There were 21,700 farm businesses in Victoria in 2019–20, accounting for 25 per cent of farm businesses in Australia. Victoria’s farm

numbers have declined by 4 per cent per annum in the past decade, falling from 32,400 in 2010–11 to 21,700 in 2019–20.

Victoria is Australia's largest producer of food and fibre products, and these make up over half of Victoria's total product exports. The state

represents 26 per cent of total Australian food and fibre export value. Victoria's food and fibre exports increased by $0.3 billion to reach a

record $14.5 billion for the 2019–20 period.
